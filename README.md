## Features by Page

### Dashboard

Your command center for daily operations.

- **Occupancy Overview** — See current occupancy rates across all properties at a glance
- **Today's Activity** — Quick view of check-ins and check-outs scheduled for today
- **Revenue Metrics** — Track total and monthly revenue with visual charts
- **Room Status Summary** — Available, occupied, maintenance, and cleaning counts
- **Recent Reservations** — Latest bookings with guest names and room details
- **Quick Actions** — One-click access to new reservations, check-ins, and check-outs
- **Hotel Comparison** — Side-by-side performance metrics for multi-property operators

### Room Management

Complete control over your room inventory.

- **Room Directory** — Comprehensive list of all rooms with status, type, and floor information
- **Status Tracking** — Mark rooms as available, occupied, under maintenance, or cleaning
- **Room Types** — Create and manage room categories with base rates and occupancy limits
- **Daily Rates** — Set individual room pricing that can override type defaults
- **Capacity Management** — Define maximum guests per room for accurate booking
- **Search & Filter** — Quickly find rooms by number, type, status, or floor
- **Notes System** — Add internal notes for housekeeping or maintenance teams

### Room Types

Define your property's accommodation offerings.

- **Type Configuration** — Create categories like Standard, Deluxe, Suite, or Presidential
- **Base Rate Setting** — Establish starting prices for each room category
- **Occupancy Limits** — Set maximum guests to prevent overbooking
- **Description Fields** — Add marketing descriptions for each room type
- **Room Count Display** — See how many rooms are assigned to each type
- **Hotel-specific Types** — Different properties can have different room type configurations

### Reservations

Streamlined booking management from inquiry to checkout.

- **Reservation Creation** — Book rooms with guest details, dates, and special requests
- **Status Workflow** — Track bookings through Pending → Confirmed → Checked In → Checked Out
- **Multi-room Bookings** — Reserve multiple rooms in a single reservation
- **Guest Selection** — Link reservations to existing guest profiles or create new ones
- **Pricing Calculation** — Automatic total calculation based on nights and room rates
- **Payment Tracking** — Record deposits, track balances, and manage payment methods
- **Cancellation Handling** — Process cancellations and no-shows with proper status updates
- **Special Requests** — Capture and display guest preferences and requirements

### Guest Management

Build lasting relationships with comprehensive guest profiles.

- **Guest Directory** — Searchable database of all guests who have stayed with you
- **Contact Information** — Store email, phone, and physical address details
- **Document Storage** — Record ID/passport numbers and document types
- **Nationality Tracking** — Useful for reporting and personalized service
- **Reservation History** — See every past and upcoming stay for each guest
- **Notes & Preferences** — Record special requirements, allergies, or preferences
- **Quick Search** — Find guests by name, email, phone, or document number

### Financial

Complete oversight of your hotel's financial health.

- **Transaction Ledger** — Record all payments, refunds, and adjustments
- **Payment Methods** — Support for cash, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and PIX
- **Status Tracking** — Monitor pending, partial, paid, and refunded transactions
- **Reservation Linking** — Connect every transaction to its associated booking
- **Reference Numbers** — Track external payment references for reconciliation
- **Date Filtering** — View transactions by day, week, month, or custom range

### Reports

Data-driven insights to inform your decisions.

- **Occupancy Reports** — Analyze room utilization over any time period
- **Revenue Reports** — Break down income by room type, property, or date range
- **Reservation Reports** — Review booking patterns and guest demographics
- **Financial Reports** — Generate statements for accounting and compliance
- **Custom Date Ranges** — Select specific periods for targeted analysis
- **Export Options** — Download reports for offline analysis or sharing

### Hotel Management

For operators managing multiple properties.

- **Property Directory** — View all hotels in your portfolio
- **Hotel Profiles** — Store name, address, contact details, and descriptions
- **Unique Codes** — Assign identifiers for internal tracking and reporting
- **Location Data** — Record city, state, country, and postal code information
- **Cross-property View** — Switch between properties or view aggregate data

### User Management

Control who has access to what.

- **User Directory** — See all team members with their roles and assigned hotels
- **Role Assignment** — Five levels from Staff to Super Admin
- **Hotel Assignment** — Restrict users to specific properties or grant chain-wide access
- **Activity Tracking** — Monitor last login times for security awareness
- **Secure Invitations** — Add new team members with proper credentials

#### Role Hierarchy

| Role | Capabilities |
|------|-------------|
| **Super Admin** | Full access to all hotels and system settings |
| **Hotel Admin** | Complete control over assigned hotel(s) |
| **Manager** | Reservations, guests, rooms, and reports |
| **Receptionist** | Check-in/out, reservations, and guest management |
| **Staff** | View-only access to assigned areas |

### Settings

Customize Staywise to match your preferences.

- **Language Selection** — Switch between English and Portuguese instantly
- **Theme Options** — Choose light mode, dark mode, or match system preference
- **General Settings** — Configure default values and system behaviors

### Profile

Personal account management for every user.

- **Personal Information** — Update name, phone, and other contact details
- **Password Security** — Change password with current password verification
- **Account Details** — View role, assigned hotel, and account creation date
- **Member Status** — See account ID and membership duration
